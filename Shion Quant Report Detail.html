01

Performance attribution 业绩归因

April closed +4.4% net of fees, ahead of SPY's +2.9% and largely driven by NVDA's +8.7% monthly contribution. The trailing 90-day Sharpe held at 1.74 despite increased dispersion in single-name returns. 4 月扣费后收益 +4.4%，跑赢 SPY 的 +2.9%，主要由 NVDA 单月 +8.7% 的贡献驱动。尽管个股收益离散度上升，近 90 日 Sharpe 仍维持在 1.74。

Top contributors followed the concentration pattern flagged in the March report: NVDA, AAPL, and MSFT together accounted for 76% of monthly equity P/L, with NVDA alone delivering 58 bps to total portfolio return. Detractors were modest in scale — LMT's defense-sector pullback (−3.2%) clipped 12 bps, and the protective put position drag amounted to a further 8 bps net of any unrealized gain on the puts themselves. 主要贡献者延续了 3 月报告所标记的集中模式：NVDA、AAPL、MSFT 三者合计贡献 4 月股票盈亏的 76%，仅 NVDA 一只就贡献了组合总收益 58 bps。拖累项规模有限——LMT 国防板块回调（−3.2%）削减 12 bps，保护性看跌仓位的净拖累再为 8 bps（含 put 自身浮盈对冲后）。

02

Beta drift attribution Beta 漂移归因

Net portfolio beta increased from 0.78 (end-March) to 0.82 (end-April), with the 30-day rolling estimate now at the upper edge of the policy band [0.65, 0.85]. 组合净 Beta 由 3 月底 0.78 上升至 4 月底 0.82，30 日滚动估计已接近政策区间 [0.65, 0.85] 的上沿。

The drift is mechanical, not behavioural — no purchases were made in April. The +0.05 contribution from NVDA is a pure mark-to-market effect: as price appreciated 8.7% over the month, NVDA's portfolio weight rose from 16.5% to 18.0%, lifting the weighted exposure to its high beta (β ≈ 1.6). The hedge offset of −0.02 reflects the SPY 530P delta, which deepens as the underlying moves up (paradoxically, the put has more delta now than at initiation because of vega and term-structure effects). 本次漂移是机械性的，而非主动操作——4 月并未进行买入。NVDA 贡献的 +0.05 完全来自市价变动效应：价格月内上涨 8.7%，使 NVDA 权重由 16.5% 升至 18.0%，进而拉高其高 Beta（β ≈ 1.6）的加权敞口。−0.02 的对冲抵消反映 SPY 530P 的 delta——随着标的上涨反而加深（看似反直觉，但因 vega 与期限结构效应所致）。

03

Hedge effectiveness 对冲有效性

The SPY 530P (8 contracts, 20 June expiry) reduced effective downside by 31% in our 5%-market-drop simulation, slightly outperforming the 25% target set in the March memo. SPY 530P（8 张合约，6/20 到期）在 5% 市场跌幅模拟中将实际下行减少 31%，略高于 3 月备忘设定的 25% 目标。

We re-ran the counterfactual with three alternative hedge constructions: do nothing, same notional via 480P (out-of-the-money), and same premium via 540P (closer-to-the-money). The 530P struck the best risk-cost trade in this quarter's volatility regime, though its margin of advantage over 540P is narrow (~3% of protection efficiency for ~8% lower premium spend). 我们以三种替代对冲方案重做反事实测算：不对冲、同名义额度 480P（深虚值）、同保费 540P（接近平值）。本季度波动率体制下 530P 实现了最佳风险-成本平衡，但相较 540P 的边际优势不大（保护效率约高 3%，保费成本约低 8%）。

Recommendations 建议

Six action items in priority order. Items 1–2 require client decision before 22 May rebalance window opens; items 3–6 are advisory and will be executed at our discretion under the standing mandate. 按优先级列出 6 项行动建议。第 1–2 项须在 5/22 再平衡窗口开启前由客户决策；第 3–6 项为顾问性建议，将在标准授权下由我方执行。

  1. Trim NVDA 200 shares pre-earnings 财报前减持 NVDA 200 股
    Restores concentration buffer. Decision required by 30 Apr 16:00 ET. See companion strategy memo of 19 Apr. 恢复集中度安全边际。须于 4/30 16:00 ET 前决定。详见 4/19 配套策略备忘。
  2. Roll SPY 530P forward to September cycle 将 SPY 530P 展期至 9 月周期
    Execute on 20 Jun OPEX regardless of underlying. Premium budget: ~$8,800 for 540P × 8. 无论标的价格如何，于 6/20 OPEX 执行。保费预算：约 $8,800（540P × 8 张）。
  3. Reduce 2Y UST allocation from 43% → 30% of bond sleeve 将 2Y UST 由债券层 43% 降至 30%
    Reallocate to 7Y duration to capture expected curve steepening. Per 15 Apr Macro Update. 转配至 7 年久期以捕捉预期的曲线变陡。详见 4/15 宏观更新。
  4. Initiate covered call ladder on AAPL 在 AAPL 启动备兑看涨梯式
    Sell 5 ct each at 230C / 240C / 250C across May / Jun / Jul. Yield enhancement on existing 1,500 sh position. 在 5/6/7 月各卖出 5 张 230C / 240C / 250C。基于现有 1,500 股 AAPL 持仓增强收益。
  5. Add small defensive position (KO or MCD) 小仓位补充防御性仓位（KO 或 MCD）
    Counterbalance Quality + Momentum tilt. Target weight 2–3% from existing cash. 对冲 Quality + Momentum 倾斜。从现金中划拨 2–3% 目标权重。
  6. Reduce digital assets to 5% target 将数字资产降至 5% 目标
    Currently 6.2% — drifted up with BTC's run. Trim 0.2 BTC, settle to USD. 当前 6.2%——随 BTC 上涨自然漂移。减持 0.2 BTC，结算为美元。
