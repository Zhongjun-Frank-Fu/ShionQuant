2026 Renewal & Scope Adjustment 2026 续约与范围调整

Parties: INTELLIGENCE INVESTMENT LLC ("Advisor") · Client 0042 ("Client")
Effective: 1 July 2026 — 30 June 2027 · Governs alongside MSL §§1–12

§1.Term合作期限

UNCHANGEDThis Addendum extends the engagement for one (1) additional year, commencing on the day after expiry of the initial term, with renewal terms substantially identical to the Master Engagement Letter dated 1 July 2024. 不变本附录将合作期限延长一（1）年，自初始期满次日起算，续约条款与 2024 年 7 月 1 日《主合作协议》基本一致。

§2.Scope · changed服务范围 · 变更

CHANGEDAdvisor's monthly retainer scope is amended to include "weekly check-in calls" "weekly check-in calls or asynchronous written briefs at Client's election, plus one (1) additional ad-hoc strategy session per quarter". All other line items in MSL §3.2 remain in effect. 变更顾问月度服务范围修订为包含 "每周例行沟通电话" "每周例行电话或客户可选的书面简报，外加每季度一次额外的非例行策略会议"。 《主合约》§3.2 其他事项继续有效。

§3.Fee · unchanged收费 · 不变

UNCHANGEDThe monthly retainer of $4,500 USD remains in effect for the renewal year. No CPI adjustment is applied. Custom Build Tier projects remain billed under separate Statement of Work as previously. 不变月度顾问费 $4,500 美元 在续约年度保持不变，不做 CPI 调整。「定制开发」等级项目仍按既有方式以单独 SOW 计费。

CHANGEDNotice of termination must be provided in writing "with thirty (30) days' notice" "with sixty (60) days' notice", reflecting the deeper integration of automated systems built under MSL §3.1. 变更解约通知须以书面形式提供 "提前 30 日" "提前 60 日"， 以反映依《主合约》§3.1 构建的自动化系统的更深度集成。
